Sustainability Initiatives

At GreenLeaf Grocers, we are committed to sustainability. Here are some of our latest initiatives:

  • Plastic Bag Ban: We have officially banned single-use plastic bags in all our stores. Customers are encouraged to bring their own reusable bags or purchase our eco-friendly alternatives.
  • Renewable Energy: We have transitioned to 100% renewable energy for all our store locations, reducing our carbon footprint significantly.
  • Community Gardens: We are supporting the creation of community gardens in urban areas to promote local food production and environmental education.
